About Our Game

What Is Wacktory

Wacktory is a fast-paced, chaotic and wacky couch co-op VR endeavor that takes advantage of the differing perspectives between VR and more traditional top-down gameplay to derive its asynchronous chaos. Here, the players take on the roles of factory workers and operators who must work together to complete increasingly complex tasks of manufacturing items in a factory full of some of the strangest products!

We are a small dev team but are exceptionally proud of what we have accomplished. We intend to bridge the gap between the traditionally single player VR experience and bring the outside world into the game! Allowing friends to jump into the fun and help add to the chaos - rather than just waiting for their turn to play.
